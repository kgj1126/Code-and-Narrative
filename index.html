Code and Digital Technology Impact on Education

How has code and digital technology changed what and how we learn? Why is it important to address? Think about the role code/CS play in our digital literacies?

I remember in Kindergarten, each class had a tech cart with iPads for each student. I barely remember what exaclty we would do on them besides some sort of learning, though. Moving up to 3rd grade, each class still had a tech cart, but now with MacBook Airs that we would practice our typing skills on. By 5th grade, we all had our own school email set up and mainly using our computers for every writing assignment. In 6th grade we got our own chromebooks that we could take home and do all of our assignments on, for the most part.

I think by us addressing our learning history with technology, we can see how advanced technology has really gotten. It's very weird to look back and see even as 5-year-olds, we were given technology to learn while in a classroom. Our generation was born into the beginnings of the digital era, but we still remember parts of our childhood without digital technology overtaking our play.
